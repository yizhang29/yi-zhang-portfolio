Risk Case Management

Poynt became a PayFac(Payment Facilitator) in order to provide a better end-to-end experience for the merchants. Merchants can just work with Poynt to be approved to run their business and they no longer need to reach out to the banks. To achieve the goal, Poynt had a partnership with Marqeta. By processing tons of prospective merchants' applications. We need to design a platform for risk analysts to review and operate on those cases. And this was the first enterprise product and the goal was to create an all-in-one solution for internal & external risk analysts to review and evaluate propective merchants to Poynt.

Timeline

  • Aug 2019 - Nov 2019

My role

  • Lead Designer: Design, User Research

Team

  • Product Manager, Engineering, Risk Analysts

Persona - External Risk Analyst

External analysts from Marqeta who could also review merchants' business profiles and coordinate with Poynt risk analysts to control the potential risks.

Persona - Poynt Risk Analyst

Poynt risk analysts & admins who could review prospective merchants' profiles. If anything is not correct, users are able to edit to approve merchants' application or decline.

Design Goal

• Simplicity made review process more efficiently so risk analysts can make descisions for plenty of prospective merchants without mistakes.
• A single place to store cases with all the relevant information;
• Secure environment to protect potential Poynt merchants' profiles.

Research

• I worked with internal analysts to reviewed their workflows and what they expected to get from a good risk management platform;
• I also iterated prototypes with external analyst from Marqeta and improved detailed UX by professional suggestions;
• Referred similar pages from other products. Limited amount of the features stop users to manage cases efficiently;
• Our original demo was so hard to understand and users spent a lot of time to figure out what does the portal provide;
• Based on all the insights I got from stakeholders and competitive research, I started from mapping out user's expectations on different workflows users will take on the portal then prioritized with my PM.

Start with mind map

• Did research on similar products & summarized the key attributes with product manager;
• Worked with PM & engineering lead to define what do we want to achieve for risk analysts and what do we want to display as a system;
• Aligned with stakeholders on vision. Our goal is to deliver an easy-to-use and polished product instead of MVP because of the risk on revenue & growth of business.
